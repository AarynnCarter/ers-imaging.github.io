ERS team members Aarynn Carter and Sasha Hinkley have been selected to host one of 15 local workshops across Europe following their attendance at the Madrid JWST Master Class in February 2020.

This workshop will be held at the University of Exeter across two consecutive days in early March 2020, with the first day focused on general JWST information and proposal planning and the second day focused on exoplanet proposal planning. More specific information will be given in the near future, for now please register your interest through the linked form below.
